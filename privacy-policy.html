Baseball Nerd

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Overview

Baseball Nerd ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal MLB companion app for Android. We are committed to being straightforward about privacy: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

The app has no user accounts and no login system.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, including:

  • Preferences — your display settings (e.g. card vs. list view, dark mode on/off)
  • XP & Level — your progress in the in-app mini-games

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Internet Access & Third-Party Data

The app connects to the internet solely to retrieve publicly available MLB data — scores, standings, stats, and player information — from the official MLB Stats API. No personal information is included in these requests. We do not operate or control the MLB Stats API; please refer to MLB's own privacy policy for information on their data practices.

Third-Party SDKs & Services

The app does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
